Practice Areas

Patents

Patents give you the right to stop competitors from making, using, selling, and importing your inventions and designs. I can help you obtain patent protection, stop others from using your patented technology, negotiate license agreements, and defend against patent infringement claims.

Trademarks

Trademarks protect your brand from confusingly similar names, logos, slogans, and trade dress. The firm can help you obtain and preserve trademarks, and defend against trademark infringement suits.

Copyrights

Copyrights give creators the exclusive right to display, distribute, reproduce, and modify their works. I can help you register copyrightable works, stop others from violating your copyright, and defend against copyright infringement claims.
