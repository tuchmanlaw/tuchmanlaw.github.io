Ido Tuchman

Ido Tuchman is the owner of The Law Office of Ido Tuchman and has been practicing intellectual property law for over ten years. Mr. Tuchman prides himself in the personal service he gives to his clients. He takes the time to carefully understand his client's needs and to explain complex legal issues in plain English.

Before starting practice on his own, Mr. Tuchman was an associate at the law firm of Merchant & Gould. Prior to entering the legal profession he worked as an engineer at Micron Technologies.

Mr. Tuchman received a J.D. degree from Franklin Pierce Law Center and a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from the State University of New York at Stony Brook.

Mr. Tuchman is admitted to practice before the United States Patent and Trademark Office, and state and federal courts of New York and Colorado.
